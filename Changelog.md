### 0.13.0

**Image:**  `quay.io/kubernetes-ingress-controller/nginx-ingress-controller:0.13.0`

*New Features:*

- NGINX 1.13.12
- Support for gRPC:
  - The annotation `nginx.ingress.kubernetes.io/grpc-backend: "true"` enable this feature
